Famous Chinese Dishes

Chinese Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Spring onions

Stir-fry eggs and vegetables, add rice, soy sauce, and toss well. Garnish with spring onions.

Pork Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • Dumpling wrappers
  • 250g minced pork
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Ginger & soy sauce
  • Spring onions

Mix pork filling with garlic, ginger, and soy sauce. Wrap in dough, steam or pan-fry until golden.

Chow Mein

Ingredients:

  • 200g egg noodles
  • 1 cup cabbage & carrots
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • Sesame oil

Boil noodles, stir-fry with vegetables and sauces. Finish with sesame oil for flavor.

Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 300g chicken breast
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • Dry red chilies
  • Peanuts
  • Soy sauce & vinegar

Stir-fry chicken, add peppers, chilies, and peanuts. Toss with soy sauce and vinegar for spicy-sour flavor.
